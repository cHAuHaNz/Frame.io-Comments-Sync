Powerful Features

Everything you need to supercharge your video review process

CSV Import

Upload pre-prepared comments with precise timecode targeting. Supports annotation flags and reply threading.

Bulk Operations

Add hundreds of comments to your timeline with a single action. Smart filtering and sequential processing.

Floating Sidebar

Persistent comment management interface that stays open while you work. Minimizable and draggable design.

Timecode Navigation

Jump directly to any timestamp in your video. Frame-accurate seeking with 30fps precision.

Smart Filtering

Filter comments by annotation status, reply type, and custom criteria. Advanced search and organization.

Audio Feedback

Success notifications with completion sounds. Visual progress indicators and real-time status updates.
